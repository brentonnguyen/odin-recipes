Bún Bò Huế Recipe

Home

Description

A spicy Vietnamese beef noodle soup originating from Huế, known for its rich broth, thick rice noodles, and fragrant lemongrass flavor. It typically includes beef shank, pork hock, and congealed pork blood, garnished with fresh herbs and lime.

Ingredients

  • Beef Shank
  • Pork Hock
  • Lemon Grass
  • Shrimp Paste
  • Chili Oil
  • Beef Broth
  • Thick Rice Noodles
  • Fish Sauce
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Banana Blossoms
  • Lime
  • Herbs

Steps

  1. Boil beef shank and pork hock, then rinse and refill with fresh clear water.
  2. Add lemongrass, onion, shrimp paste, and fish sauce and simmer for 2 hours.
  3. Prepare thick rice noodles by boiling and draining them.
  4. Make chili oil by frying chili flakes with garlic.
  5. Assemble bowl with noodles, sliced meat, broth, and garnish with herbs, lime, and banana blossoms.
