Phở Recipe

Home

Description

Vietnam’s most famous noodle soup, featuring a fragrant broth made from slow-simmered beef or chicken bones, star anise, cinnamon, and other spices, served with rice noodles and thinly sliced meat.

Ingredients

  • Beef / Chicken (either one)
  • Rice Noodles
  • Beef/Chicken bones
  • Star Anise
  • Cinnamon
  • Cloves
  • Onions
  • Ginger
  • Fish Sauce
  • Sugar
  • Bean Sprouts
  • Basil
  • Lime

Steps

  1. Roast onion and ginger, then simmer with beef/chicken bones, star anise, and cinnamon for 4 hours.
  2. Strain broth, season with fish sauce and sugar.
  3. Cook rice noodles separately and slice meat thinly.
  4. Place noodles and raw beef or cooked chicken in a bowl, then pour hot broth over.
  5. Serve with basil, bean sprouts, lime, and chili.
