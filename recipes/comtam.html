Cơm Tấm Recipe

Home

Description

A classic Vietnamese broken rice dish, usually served with grilled pork (sườn), shredded pork skin, and a fried egg, accompanied by pickled vegetables and fish sauce.

Ingredients

  • Broken Rice
  • Pork Chops
  • Fish Sauce
  • Sugar
  • Garlic
  • Shallots
  • Pickled Carrots
  • Pickled Daikon
  • Scallion Oil
  • Fried Egg
  • Cucumber

Steps

  1. Marinate pork chops with fish sauce, sugar, garlic, and shallots.
  2. Grill pork until caramelized.
  3. Cook broken rice and prepare pickled carrots and daikon.
  4. Fry an egg and drizzle scallion oil over rice.
  5. Serve with grilled pork, egg, pickles, and fish sauce.
Broken rice, pork chops, fish sauce, sugar, garlic, shallots, pickled carrots and daikon, scallion oil, fried egg, cucumber.
