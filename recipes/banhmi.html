Bánh Mì Recipe

Home

Description

A crispy Vietnamese baguette sandwich filled with meat, pâté, and pickled veggies.

Ingredients

  • Baguette
  • Pork/Chicken/Tofu
  • Pâté
  • Mayonnaise
  • Pickled Carrots
  • Pickled Daikon
  • Cucumber
  • Cilantro
  • Chili
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Slice baguette and lightly toast it.
  2. Spread mayonnaise and pâté inside.
  3. Fill with grilled pork, chicken, or tofu.
  4. Add pickled carrots, daikon, cucumber, cilantro, and chili.
  5. Drizzle with soy sauce and serve.
